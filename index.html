Research

I am a PhD student in the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University. I am co-advised by Prof. Siddhartha Srinivasa at Personal Robotics Lab and Prof. Matthew T. Mason at Manipulation Lab. My primary research interest is learning and planning manipulation.

As an undergraduate, I studied EECS and Mathematics at MIT, and interned at Canon Reserach Center and Animation Technology Center at Walth Disney Animation Studios. Upon graduation, I worked at Dreamworks Animation Studios as a Technical Director for Kung Fu Panda 2 production and worked at an Entertainment VC as an investment analyst before going back to MIT for master's degree. During my master's, I was co-advised by Leslie Pack Kaelbling and Tomas Lozano-Perez.

I am a receipient of Kwanjeong Educational Scholarship and Presidential Fellowship at CMU School of Computer Science.
